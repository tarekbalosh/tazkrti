Egypt to hold 29th arab music festival

23 azir avenu

Egypt is set to host the 29th Arab Music Festival, celebrating the richness and diversity of Arabic music and culture. Held at the Cairo Opera House and various venues across Egypt, the festival will feature performances by renowned artists from across the Arab world. This annual event, organized by the Egyptian Ministry of Culture, aims to preserve traditional Arabic music while embracing contemporary expressions, fostering a deeper appreciation for the region's artistic heritage.
